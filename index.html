About Aloha Cleaning Co.

Welcome to Aloha Cleaning Co. We pride ourselves on meticulous attention to detail and friendly service.

Aloha! We are the Alexander family and the proud owners of Aloha Cleaning Co! We are a family owned business specializing in residential, commercial, airbnb and post construction cleaning in the Louisville and southern Indian area. We moved to Kentucky April of 2020 and shortly after began our cleaning business journey. We take pride in maintaining the aloha spirit and culture not just within our family but with those we cross paths with. Akahai", meaning kindness to be expressed with tenderness; "Lōkahi", meaning unity, to be expressed with harmony; "ʻOluʻolu" meaning agreeable, to be expressed with pleasantness; "Haʻahaʻa", meaning humility, to be expressed with modesty; "Ahonui", meaning patience, to be expressed with perseverance With a like minded ever growing team of professional cleaners we aim to provide cleaning services that leave you at peace and relaxed. We value the importance of professionalism and providing excellent customer service.
